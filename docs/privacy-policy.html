Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 20, 2026

What We Collect

When you interact with BratBot on Discord, we receive your Discord user ID and message content. This data is used solely to generate a reply and is not stored permanently.

How We Use Your Data

Your message is sent to our AI chatbot to generate a response, which is sent back to you via Discord. We do not sell, share, or use your data for marketing purposes.

Data Retention

Message content is processed in real time and is not retained after the response is sent. User IDs may be temporarily stored for rate limiting purposes (up to 60 seconds).

Third Parties

We use Discord's API to send and receive messages. Discord's privacy policy applies to their handling of message data.

Contact

For questions about this policy, open an issue on our GitHub repository.
